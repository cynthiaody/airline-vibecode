Baggage Information

Review our baggage policies before your trip to ensure a smooth airport experience.

👜

Cabin Baggage

Bring one cabin bag weighing up to 7kg, along with one small personal item such as a handbag or laptop bag.

🧳

Checked Baggage

Economy passengers receive up to 23kg per checked bag, while premium cabins enjoy higher baggage allowances.

📦

Oversized Baggage

Oversized or overweight baggage may incur additional charges and must comply with airline size restrictions.

🚲

Sports Equipment

Golf clubs, bicycles, skis and other sporting equipment are accepted with advance booking and applicable fees.

Airport Check-in Guide

Follow these steps to make your airport experience quick, easy and stress-free.

01

Online Check-in

Check in online from 24 hours before departure. Select your seat, confirm your details and download your boarding pass.

02

Arrive Early

Arrive at least 3 hours before international flights and 2 hours before domestic flights.

03

Baggage Drop

If you have checked baggage, visit the baggage drop counter after completing online check-in.

04

Boarding Gate

Proceed to your gate after security screening and ensure you arrive before the boarding deadline.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about travelling with Global Airline.

How early should I arrive at the airport?

We recommend arriving three hours before international flights and two hours before domestic flights.

Can I change my travel date?

Yes. Flight changes are available depending on your ticket conditions and seat availability.

How do I check my flight status?

You can check your flight status through our website, mobile app or customer support team.

What documents do I need to travel?

Passengers should carry a valid passport, visa where required, boarding pass and any required health documents.
