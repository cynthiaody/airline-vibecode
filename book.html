Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most common travel questions.

Can I change my booking after payment?

Yes. Most bookings can be modified through the Manage Booking page, subject to fare conditions.

How much baggage can I carry?

Baggage allowance depends on your ticket class and destination. Full details are available on the Travel Information page.

When does online check-in open?

Online check-in opens 24 hours before departure and closes 90 minutes before takeoff.

Can I request a special meal?

Yes. Vegetarian, vegan, halal, kosher and medical meal options are available when requested before your flight.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes. You can purchase travel insurance during booking or later through your reservation.
